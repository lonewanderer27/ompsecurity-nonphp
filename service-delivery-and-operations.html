Service Delivery and Operations

We accomplish efficient and effective operations through the following stages:

Transition Period

Area Security Survey and Inspection

Mounting of Security Personnel

Quality Checks and Controls

Taking Feedback from Clients

Supervision and Inspection

OMP takes pride in having a consistent night and day supervision and inspection of posted security personnel. We have a dedicated Field Officers and Area Inspectors with direct control of posted guards that renders a regular report on the performance of each post

Equipment and Sales

OMP may purchase any equipment necessary as required and provided in the contract of service with the client.

Post Rotation of Guards

To prevent the possibility of complacency and avoid over familiarization, the guards are rotated on regular basis. However, in case of specific request from the client, a guard may be permitted to continue over a specified time.
